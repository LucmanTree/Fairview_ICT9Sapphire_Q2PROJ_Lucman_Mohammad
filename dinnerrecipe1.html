Piaparan a Bakas

Description

Fish covered in coconut meat with a side of soup

Ingredients

  • Palapa
  • Cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic sliced
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1/2 cup palapa
  • 1-2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 750 ml fresh coconut milk
  • 250 ml fresh coconut cream
  • 1 kilo Bakas smoked tuna, cut into chunks
  • pring onions sliced
    • For the papar:
  • Cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic sliced
  • 1 tomato sliced
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper sliced
  • 1/4 cup of palapa
  • 1-2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 2 cups of fresh grated coconut meat
  • 1/4 cup from the soup
  • spring onions sliced

Steps

  1. Grill the fish 4 minutes on each side in charcoal
  2. While it's grilling on a wok add shalots and Palapa with turmeric stir till fragrant
  3. Add coconut milk with red capsicum and bring to a simmer
  4. Add fish, pechay and coconut cream, continue to simme for 10 minutes
  5. Add coconut milk then cook for another 5 minutes. Add spring onions then turn heat off.
  6. Season with Fish Sauce and ground black pepper
