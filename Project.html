Lutong Moro

Discover the various flavors of the Moros of Mindanao

Moro cuisine is a rich and diverse culinary tradition that originates from the Moro people of Mindanao, Sulu, and Palawan in the Philippines. Deeply rooted in the cultural and religious practices of the predominantly Muslim Moro communities, the cuisine is characterized by its use of bold flavors, vibrant spices, and halal preparation methods. It reflects centuries of trade and cultural exchange with Malay, Arab, Indian, and Southeast Asian influences.
