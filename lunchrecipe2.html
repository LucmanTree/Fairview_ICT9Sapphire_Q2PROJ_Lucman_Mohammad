Piaparan

Description

Chicken that has its own soup and dry version

Ingredients

  • Palapa
  • 1 1/2 kg chicken legs
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 6 cups water
  • chayote, sliced
  • 3 stalks spring onions, chopped
  • oil
  • salt
  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 green capsicum, sliced
  • 1/2 red capsicum, sliced
  • oil

Steps

  1. In a large mortar combine all palapa ingredients together, pound it with a pestle until its paste like in consistency. Set it aside
  2. Prepare a pot, over stove top on medium heat add oil then sauté garlic and onions.
  3. Add a tablespoon of the palapa continue to sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken and salt, slightly brown it on all sides then pour water. Bring it to a boil then simmer for 25 minutes. Remove chicken from the pot then set
  5. Add chayote to the pot then cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add coconut milk then cook for another 5 minutes. Add spring onions then turn heat off.
  7. While soup is boiling, in a wok heat oil then sauté the remaining palapa.
  8. Add turmeric, shredded coconut, red capsicum and green capsicum. Stir fry until the shredded coconut is coated with the turmeric evenly.
  9. Add the chicken to the coconut mixture, mix well to coat chicken with the coconut. Cover the wok, continue to cook in low heat for 10 minutes.
  10. Place chicken and coconut in a serving platter then serve it with the soup on the side.
