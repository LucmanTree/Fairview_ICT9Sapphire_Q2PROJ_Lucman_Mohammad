Palapa

Description

The flavorful paste that is the base for many dishes. It is also added to anything as a sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 bundles about 2 kg Sakurab, cleaned and dried
  • 4 thumb-sized ginger sliced
  • 4 tbsp birds eye chili depending on the level of spiciness you’d want
  • Salt
  • 1 cup cooking oil you can keep adding up to 1.5 cups
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1-2 bulbs garlic chopped

Steps

  1. In a food processor, blend sliced sakurab, ginger, and chilis. Process in batches if needed, then mix the batches in a container.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sauté chopped garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the sakurab mixture and optional turmeric powder for color. Cook for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, until the mixture changes color or is cooked.
  4. Season with salt and add more oil if desired.
  5. Let the palapa cool completely before transferring it to a dry container.
